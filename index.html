About the venture
Food for Thoth is an online platform that takes you back to the lost art of passing on books once you’ve read them. Sharing and exchanging books has now become a thing of the past. For our book lovers, we aim to bring back the feel of reading a used book, holding it in your hands and reading the same story through your eyes that several before you have read.

The Name Explained
Thoth is the Egyptian god of Wisdom. Food for Thoth, as the name suggests, is FFT trying to bring back lost wisdom through the old practice of sharing it. Share your knowledge, someone learns while you earn!

How it Works
When you sign up, you will first be asked to choose genres that you would like to read. Based on your preferences, books will show up on your recommended feed. Should you choose to borrow a book, all you have to do is swipe right on it, to send a message to the lender to let them know you’re interested. You will then be asked to pick a lending period, the pricing of which is explained in the Pricing Model ahead. Based on what you choose, you pay the amount online and confirm your rental. An FFT executive will then be assigned to deliver the book from the lender’s address to you, the borrower.

How it Works
You can also make some easy money from the comfort of your home by becoming a lender on FFT. All you have to do is take a picture of the book you want to lend and upload it under the correct genre along with the author’s name. Your book will show up on the recommended feed of someone who is interested in the genre. Should they choose to swipe right, they will be asked to choose a borrowing period. According to the plan they pick, they will be making an instant online transfer to FFT. We get a small commission, while you receive the payment instantly, following which, an FFT executive picks up the book and drops it back to your place when the lending period is at an end.
